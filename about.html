About Us

Welcome to Alfred's Cafe — your neighborhood destination for freshly prepared meals and cozy vibes.

Our Story

Founded in 2024 in the heart of Nairobi, Alfred’s Cafe was born out of a passion for good food and great company. Our goal has always been to serve affordable, locally sourced meals in a space where everyone feels at home.

Why Choose Us?

  • ✅ Fresh, high-quality ingredients
  • ✅ Friendly and welcoming environment
  • ✅ Affordable prices for everyone
  • ✅ Fast delivery and excellent service

Meet the Owner

Alfred Ndivo, a passionate entrepreneur and food lover, started the cafe with a mission to bring people together over great food. He believes in giving back to the community and supporting local farmers and businesses.
